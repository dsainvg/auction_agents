## Early Life and Background

Kartik Sharma was born on August 5, 2002, in the vibrant city of Delhi, India. From a young age, he exhibited a passion for sports, particularly cricket. Growing up in a family that cherished the game, it was only natural for him to be drawn to the cricket field. His father, a former cricketer at the local level, played a pivotal role in nurturing his talent.

Sharma's early education at St. Stephen's School provided him with a solid foundation, not just academically but also in terms of sportsmanship. It was during these formative years that he honed his skills, participating in various local tournaments and gaining invaluable experience. His dedication and hard work soon began to pay off, leading him to be recognized as a promising cricketer.

## Cricketing Journey

Sharma's professional cricket journey began when he joined the Delhi U-19 team. His performances were nothing short of spectacular, showcasing his ability to adapt to various formats of the game. In the 2019-2020 season, he made headlines during the U-19 National Championship, where he was the leading wicket-taker for his team. His ability to swing the ball and take crucial wickets made him a standout player.

### Breakthrough in Domestic Cricket

After his success in the U-19 circuit, Kartik Sharma's big break came when he was selected for the Delhi Ranji Trophy team. In his debut match, Sharma made an immediate impact, taking three wickets and helping his team secure a vital victory.

His impressive performances in the domestic circuit have not gone unnoticed. Cricket analysts and fans have started to draw comparisons between Sharma and some of the greats of the game.
