BIOGRAPHY
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
    Hailing from Jharkhand, Anukul Roy is a left-arm spin bowler who is also
  handy with the bat. The 26-year-old rose to fame, when he finished as the
  joint-leading wicket-taker at the U-19 World Cup in 2018. Anukul over the
  years has shown he is a livewire on the field and a handy cricketer and it
  will be interesting to see how KKR use him in their scheme of things.
