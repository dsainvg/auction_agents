BIOGRAPHY
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
    Zeeshan Ansari is a young leg-break bowler from Uttar Pradesh. The
  youngster climbed up the ranks with performances at the U19 level and
  domestic level that helped him secure an INR 40 Lakh deal with SRH for TATA
  IPL 2025. It will be a great opportunity for the leggie to play alongside
  one of the best leg spinners like Adam Zampa and Rahul Chahar in the SRH
  lineup.
