BIOGRAPHY
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
  The pacer from Saurashtra will be looking forward to his maiden IPL
  season after he was bought by RR for INR 1.20 Cr during the player auction
  ahead of the IPL 2021. The youngster will be coming into this year’s IPL on
  the back of some impressive performances in the Syed Mushtaq Ali Trophy and
  Vijay Hazare Trophy and will be keen to make a for RR.
