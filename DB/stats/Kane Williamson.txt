BIOGRAPHY
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
    Part of the famed ‘Fab Four’ along with Virat Kohli, Steven Smith and Joe
  Root, Kane Williamson is one of the coolest customers around the circuit. He
  rose to the occasion wonderfully well in the 2018 IPL as he captained SRH to
  the final, in the absence of regular captain David Warner. Williamson’s
  abilities with the bat are nothing short of substantial as he has scored
  runs across conditions and formats. With Sunrisers having a formidable set
  of foreign players in their playing XI, Williamson often has to wait for his
  opportunities, but when he does, he makes the most of it.
