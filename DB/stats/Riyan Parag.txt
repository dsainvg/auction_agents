BIOGRAPHY
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
    A member of India’s 2018 U19 World Cup-winning squad, Riyan Parag was
  bought by Rajasthan Royals for INR 20 lakh in the third and final round of
  the player auction before IPL 2019. Son of former Assam state cricketer
  Parag Das and national record holder swimmer Mithu Baruah, Riyan can hit the
  ball hard. The right-handed batsman made his IPL debut in 2019 and now holds
  the record of being the youngest to score an IPL fifty. While he did not
  score many runs in the IPL 2020 and even in 2021, RR have shown faith in his
  abilities by buying him back at the mega auction for a whopping INR
  3,80,00,000. Now that the two are reunited again, Riyan will be required to
  play a bigger role.
