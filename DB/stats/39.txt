BIOGRAPHY
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
  Delhi Capitals brought in Anrich Nortje as a replacement for Chris Woakes
  ahead of IPL 2020 and the South African speedster, in his maiden IPL season,
  delivered the goods for Delhi Capitals, scalping as many as 22 wickets. The
  right-arm pacer, who has played 10 Tests, 9 ODIs and 5 T20Is, will once
  again be eager to make his presence felt as Delhi Capitals look to make
  their mark in the tournament.
