BIOGRAPHY
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
    A key member of India’s Under 19 World Cup 2020, Kartik Tyagi picked up
  11 wickets during India’s run to the final. The right-arm pacer impressed
  one and all with his ability to move the ball around with good pace during
  the tournament. Rajasthan Royals bought Tyagi for INR 1.30 crores during the
  IPL 2020 Player Auction and went on to pick 9 wickets in his first season of
  the tournament. The right-arm pacer has since travelled to Australia as a
  net bowler with the Indian team and will be looking to put into practice
  whatever he has learnt from that stint in the IPL 2021.
