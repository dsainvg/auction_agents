BIOGRAPHY
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
    A slow left-arm orthodox spinner from Tamil Nadu, R. Sai Kishore is known
  for his control, height advantage, and economical bowling. With over 350
  domestic wickets, he has been a consistent performer in Indian domestic
  cricket. Sai Kishore was previously part of CSK in 2020-21 before moving to
  Gujarat Titans in 2022, where he played a role in their title-winning
  campaign. GT used their RTM (Right to Match) card to secure him for INR 2
  Crore at the 2025 TATA IPL Auction.
