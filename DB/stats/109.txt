BIOGRAPHY
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
    Ashwani Kumar is a young left-arm fast bowler from Punjab. He garnered
  attention with his ability to bowl brilliantly in the slog overs. He has
  plenty of variations in his kitty which makes him a promising asset. MI
  roped him in for INR 30 Lakh at the 2025 TATA IPL Auction.
