BIOGRAPHY
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
   ïˆ² Shahrukh Khan, a tall hard-hitting batter from Tamil Nadu, is known for
  his explosive batting and occasional off-spin bowling. His ability to
  deliver late cameos and target bowlers during the slog overs made him a
  standout player in the IPL. He gained prominence in IPL 2021, where Punjab
  Kings picked him for INR 5.25 Crore. Following seasons in 2022 and 2023 with
  Punjab (INR 9 Crore), Shahrukh moved to Gujarat Titans in 2024 and was then
  retained for TATA IPL 2025 due to his match-winning abilities in the lower
  order.
