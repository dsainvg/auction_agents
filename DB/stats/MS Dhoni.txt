BIOGRAPHY
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
  A living legend of the game, MS Dhoni will once again don the CSK jersey
  in TATA IPL 2025. The five-time IPL-winning captain, known for his calm
  demeanour and astute leadership, continues to be a pillar of strength for
  the franchise. Dhoni has amassed over 5000 runs and effected close to 200
  dismissals behind the stumps. While his role as a batter has evolved over
  the years, his ability to finish games remains unmatched. Retained as an
  uncapped player, the CSK talisman will be crucial both as a mentor and a
  player as the franchise aims for a record sixth IPL title.
