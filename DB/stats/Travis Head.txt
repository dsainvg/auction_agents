  in world cricket and can even bowl handy off-spin. Head is known for
  stepping up on big occasions as reflected by his match-winning centuries in
  the World Test Championship 2021-23 final and ODI World Cup 2023 final. The
  Australian opener strikes at over 160 in T20Is and over 170 in the IPL. He

  567 runs in 15 games including one century as well as a joint-fastest fifty
