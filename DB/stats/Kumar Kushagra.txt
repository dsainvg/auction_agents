BIOGRAPHY
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
    A promising wicketkeeper-batter from Jharkhand, Kumar Kushagra was part
  of India’s runners-up finish in the U19 World Cup 2022. He made headlines in
  domestic cricket with a double century in his maiden Ranji Trophy season.
  His performances earned him a massive INR 7.20 Crore deal with Delhi
  Capitals in IPL 2024. For TATA IPL 2025, Gujarat Titans secured his services
  for INR 65 Lakh, adding depth to their wicketkeeping options.
