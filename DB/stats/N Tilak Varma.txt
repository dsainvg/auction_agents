  auction and was straightaway drafted into the team, making an instant
  impact, scoring 397 runs at an average of 36.09 and a strike rate of 131.02.
  He bettered that performance the next year, scoring 343 at an average of
  42.88, striking at 164.11, a performance that also earned him a national
  debut. In IPL 2024, Tilak put up a destructive performance, scoring 416
  runs. The player was retained by MI ahead of the 2025 TATA IPL 2025 Auction
  and comes in red-hot form recently having become the first ever player to
  score three successive 100s in T20s.
