BIOGRAPHY
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
    Hailing from Uttarakhand, Anuj Rawat is a left-handed wicketkeeper-batter
  who moved to Delhi at the age of 11 to pursue cricket. Known for his
  powerful on-side hitting, Rawat has previously played for Rajasthan Royals
  and Royal Challengers Bengaluru in the IPL. Gujarat Titans added depth to
  their squad by securing his services for INR 30 Lakh at the 2025 TATA IPL
  Auction.
