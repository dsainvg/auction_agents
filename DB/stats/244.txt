BIOGRAPHY
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
    A left-arm spinner, Mitchell Santner has been a regular in the IPL,
  spending seven years at Chennai Super Kings and winning two titles
  in-process. Mitchell Santner has also been a consistent performer for New
  Zealand at the international level and led his side to finish runners-up at
  the 2025 ICC Champions Trophy. He was signed by MI for INR 2 Crore. Mitchell
  Santner is also very handy with the bat and is capable of playing a quick
  knock.
