BIOGRAPHY
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
  One of India’s premier fast bowlers, Mohammed Siraj has built a
  reputation for swinging the new ball and striking early in the powerplay.
  The Hyderabad-born pacer made his IPL debut for Sunrisers Hyderabad in 2017
  before moving to Royal Challengers Bengaluru in 2018, where he became a
  mainstay in their bowling attack. Siraj remains a high-impact player with
  close to 200 international wickets and being a part of India’s T20 World Cup
  2024-winning campaign. Gujarat Titans invested heavily in him, securing his
  services for a staggering INR 12.25 Crore at the 2025 TATA IPL Auction.
