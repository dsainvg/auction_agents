BIOGRAPHY
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
    A tall left-arm pacer who can swing the ball at high speeds, Nandre
  Burger was picked by Rajasthan Royals (RR) for INR 3.5 crore as an injury
  replacement for Sandeep Sharma during IPL 2025. Coincidentally, it was RR
  who gave the South African fast bowler his IPL debut in 2024, and he picked
  up seven wickets in six games at a healthy average of 20.71. Despite injury
  troubles, Burger’s consistent performances in domestic cricket for a number
  of years led to his international debut in December 2023. He went on to
  represent SA across formats, all within the space of two weeks.
