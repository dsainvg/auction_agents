BIOGRAPHY
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
  A cornerstone of CSK’s success, Ravindra Jadeja has been instrumental in
  the franchise’s success in the IPL over the years. The star all-rounder is
  known for his economical left-arm orthodox bowling in the middle overs and
  his exploits with the bat in the slog overs. His heroics in the IPL 2023
  final, where he clinched victory with 10 runs off the last two balls, remain
  one of the most iconic moments in CSK history. Fresh from winning the T20
  World Cup 2024 and the Champions Trophy 2025 with India, Jadeja will once
  again be a crucial player for CSK in their quest for another title. He was
  retained ahead of TATA IPL 2025.
