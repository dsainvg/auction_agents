BIOGRAPHY
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
    A power-packed hitter also known for his wicket-keeping skills, Ryan
  Rickelton is a South African international who has gained the spotlight,
  having displayed scintillating form across various formats in recent times.
  The player was a part of South Africa’s 2024 T20 World Cup squad, where they
  finished runners-up, and most recently scored a 103-run knock for his team
  during their Champions Trophy 2025 opener against Afghanistan. The left
  hander attracted attention due to his ability to score quickly and was
  picked by MI for INR 1 Crore at the 2025 TATA IPL Auction.
