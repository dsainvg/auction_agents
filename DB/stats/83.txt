BIOGRAPHY
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
    A traditional slow left-arm orthodox spinner, Manav Suthar is known for
  his variations and ability to deceive batters with flight and turn. The
  Rajasthan-based spinner gained recognition after bagging 39 wickets in just
  six matches in the Ranji Trophy 2022/23. He was part of Gujarat Titans in
  IPL 2024, and the franchise showed continued faith in his abilities by
  re-signing him for INR 30 Lakh at the TATA IPL 2025 Auction.
