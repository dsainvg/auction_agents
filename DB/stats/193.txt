BIOGRAPHY
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
  Captain of Kerala’s limited-overs side, Sachin Baby scored heavily in the
  2012-13 Vijay Hazare Trophy, India’s premier domestic 50-over tournament.
  His century in the quarter-final earned Kerala a place in the final four of
  the tournament. Baby’s penchant for limited-overs cricket earned him a
  contract with the Rajasthan Royals for IPL 2013. RCB picked him up in the
  IPL Player Auction 2016. In 2018, the southpaw represented SRH and in the
  IPL 2021 Player Auction he was bought by the RCB for his base price of INR
  20 lakhs
