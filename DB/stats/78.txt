BIOGRAPHY
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
    An experienced campaigner, Shreyas Gopal is a utility player who brings a
  lot to the table. An aggressive leg-spinner who can pick up wickets in the
  middle overs, Gopal can provide crucial contributions with the bat in the
  lower-middle order as well. He is one of those rare bowlers to have claimed
  an IPL hat-trick, with his scalps including Virat Kohli, AB de Villiers, and
  Marcus Stoinis. The Karnataka-born leg-spinner was part of the Mumbai
  Indians squad that lifted the IPL trophy in 2015. CSK secured him at his
  base price of INR 30 Lakh at the 2025 TATA IPL Auction.
