BIOGRAPHY
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
  Delhi Capitals brought Daniel Sams to their squad for IPL 2020 as a
  replacement for an injured Jason Roy. The left-arm pacer, representing
  Sydney Thunder, was the leading wicket-taker in the Big Bash League 2019-20
  and subsequently earned a call-up to Australia’s limited-overs squad for the
  England tour. Sams’ addition will add more firepower to Delhi Capitals’
  pace-bowling attack which includes the likes of Kagiso Rabada, Ishant
  Sharma, Keemo Paul, Anrich Nortje and Mohit Sharma among others.
