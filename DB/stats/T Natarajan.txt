BIOGRAPHY
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
    Tamil Nadu’s Thangarasu Natarajan made a name for himself with his
  ability to bowl lethal yorkers. The left-arm pacer made his IPL debut for
  Punjab Kings in 2017 and then moved to Sunrisers Hyderabad in the 2018
  season. The Tamil Nadu bowler also debuted for India across formats during
  the Australian tour in 2020/21. IPL 2024 was his best season where he
  managed to collect 19 wickets to help SRH reach the final. Delhi Capitals
  acquired his services for the TATA IPL 2025 at a massive price of INR 10.75
  Crore.
