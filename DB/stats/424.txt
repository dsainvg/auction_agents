BIOGRAPHY
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
    Vidarbha's all-rounder Harsh Dubey joined Sunrisers Hyderabad as a
  replacement for R Smaran in TATA IPL 2025. Dubey, a slow left-arm orthodox
  spinner, made headlines in the Ranji Trophy 2024/25 season with a
  record-breaking performance. He secured 69 wickets in the season - an
  all-time record in the history of the Ranji Trophy - to guide Vidarbha to
  the title.
