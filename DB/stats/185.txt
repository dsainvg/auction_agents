BIOGRAPHY
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
    Jaydev Unadkat, an experienced left-arm pacer from Saurashtra, has been a
  consistent presence in the IPL since 2010. He made his debut with KKR,
  before playing for multiple franchises such as RCB, DC, RPS, RR, MI, LSG,
  and SRH. Unadkat was a pivotal figure for RPS in 2017, claiming 24 wickets
  in 12 matches, helping the team reach the finals. His strong performances in
  the IPL earned him an INR 11.5 Crore deal with RR in 2018. Known for his
  deceptive off-cutters, Unadkat returns to SRH for TATA IPL 2025 after being
  acquired for INR 1 Crore.
