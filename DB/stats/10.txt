BIOGRAPHY
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
  One of India’s most reliable and versatile wicketkeeper-batters, KL Rahul
  played a pivotal role in India’s Champions Trophy 2025 title win. A
  consistent IPL performer, he has crossed the 500-run mark in six different
  seasons and holds the record for the joint-second fastest IPL fifty (14
  balls). Known for his ability to accelerate at will, Delhi Capitals made him
  their most expensive buy at INR 14 Crore for TATA IPL 2025.
