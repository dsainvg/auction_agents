BIOGRAPHY
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
    A versatile left-handed batter and slow left-arm orthodox bowler, Shahbaz
  Ahamad is a handy all-rounder to have. Shahbaz spent four years with RCB
  from 2020 before being traded to SRH for IPL 2024. Shahbaz is also a regular
  for the Bengal T20 side and has played crucial knocks with the bat
  time-in-time again while shining with the ball. One of Shahbaz’s best
  matches in the IPL came in last season as he helped SRH reach the finals,
  scoring 18 runs and backing it up with three wickets in the Qualifier. In
  IPL 2025, LSG picked him up for INR 2.4 Crore.
