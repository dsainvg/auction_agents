BIOGRAPHY
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
    A left-arm spinner and a handy lower-order batsman from Delhi, Pawan Negi
  is an ideal foil for the T20 format. He was picked by the Delhi Daredevils
  (now Delhi Capitals) while still a teenager and showed promise with his
  all-round abilities. Ahead of the 2016 season, Delhi Daredevils picked him
  at the Auction for Rs 8.5 crores. It came at a time when he was awarded with
  a call-up for the Indian team in the T20 format – making his debut against
  the United Arab Emirates (UAE) in the Asia Cup, 2016. Negi was also a part
  of India’s squad at the T20 World Cup. Since the 2017 season, Negi was a
  part of the Royal Challengers Bangalore. Kolkata Knight Riders picked him
  ahead of the 2021 season.
