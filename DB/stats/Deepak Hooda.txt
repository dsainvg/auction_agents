--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

  striker of the cricket ball, has been in the know for the Sunrisers of late.

  recognised and the franchise sees some potential in the youngster. He has

  innings with the bat and can quickly help recover the team in case of early
  wickets. If he fires at the right time, the PBKS batting unit will be a
  settled one in the 2021 season.
