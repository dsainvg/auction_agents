BIOGRAPHY
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
  Sri Lankan all-rounder, Wanindu Hasaranga comes in as a replacement for
  Adam Zampa in the remainder of IPL 2021. Wanindu was drafted straight into
  the senior men’s side from Sri Lanka’s U-19 team. An explosive middle-order
  batsman, Wanindu is also useful with his leggies. He had a dream start to
  his international career when he claimed a hat-trick in his very first
  outing in an ODI in June 2017. Wanindu is all set to don the RCB colours and
  will add the right balance to the squad.
