BIOGRAPHY
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
    Surya Kumar Yadav came to the spotlight with his performance at the
  2011-12 Ranji Trophy season where he topped the charts for Mumbai scoring
  754 runs in 9 games. The same year, Surya Kumar was picked by Mumbai
  Indians. In 2013, he moved to KKR where he played a crucial role in the
  middle order scoring quick runs and was part of the team that won the trophy
  in 2014. In 2018, SKY moved back to MI and had a breakthrough season scoring
  512 runs. He was also instrumental in the team’s success in 2019 and 2020
  which eventually saw him get a national team call-up. Since then there has
  been no looking back for Surya Kumar Yadav, who has become a consistent
  performer for both his IPL franchise and the Indian team and was part of the
  team that won the 2024 T20 World Cup. Surya currently is also the captain of
  the Indian National T20 side taking over the role after Rohit Sharma’s
  retirement from the format. His ability to score quickly with his innovative
  stroke play and deliver consistently saw him being retained by MI for TATA
  IPL 2025.
