BIOGRAPHY
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
    Known for his explosive finishing ability and handy leg spin, Rahul
  Tewatia has been a match-winner in IPL, delivering clutch performances under
  pressure. His five sixes in an over against Sheldon Cottrell in IPL 2020 and
  a last-over heist in IPL 2022 (12 off 2 balls) are among the most iconic
  moments in the tournament’s history. Having represented RR, PBKS, DC, and GT
  over the years, Tewatia played a crucial role in GT’s IPL 2022 title win and
  was retained for TATA IPL 2025.
