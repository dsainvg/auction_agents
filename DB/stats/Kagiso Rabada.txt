BIOGRAPHY
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
   ïˆ² One of the most lethal fast bowlers in world cricket, Kagiso Rabada is
  known for consistently clocking speeds above 140 kmph and delivering
  match-winning performances. With over 200 international appearances for
  South Africa and more than 550 wickets across formats, his ability to turn
  games single-handedly is well-documented. He has been a dominant force in
  the IPL as well, picking up 100+ wickets while playing for Delhi Capitals
  (2017-2021) and Punjab Kings (2022-2024). His best IPL season came in 2020,
  where he scalped 30 wickets in 17 matches to win the Purple Cap and help DC
  reach their first-ever IPL final. To strengthen their bowling attack,
  Gujarat Titans secured Rabada for INR 10.75 Crore ahead of TATA IPL 2025.
