BIOGRAPHY
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
    A seasoned campaigner with over 400 international wickets, Ishant Sharma
  has been a mainstay in the IPL since its inception in 2008. The Delhi-born
  pacer possesses the ability to swing the new ball both ways. Having played
  for six different IPL teams throughout his career, he brings a wealth of
  experience to Gujarat Titans, who picked him up for INR 75 Lakh at the 2025
  TATA IPL Auction. His presence will be invaluable, both as a strike bowler
  and a mentor for younger pacers in the squad.
