--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

  appearances across all formats for Sri Lanka. After stints with Rajasthan
  Royals (2018) and RCB (2021), he got a full-fledged IPL season with Lucknow
  Super Giants in 2022, claiming nine wickets in 12 matches. He was later a
