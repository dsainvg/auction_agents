BIOGRAPHY
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
    Raj Angad Bawa was one of the key members during India’s triumph at the
  2022 U-19 World Cup. The youngster was effective with both the bat and the
  ball, scoring 252 runs in just five innings, including a whirlwind knock of
  162* against Uganda while picking 9 wickets, including a fifer in the final
  against England. The Himachali, following his performances, was picked by
  PBKS for IPL 2022 and will now don the Blue and Gold after being picked by
  MI for INR 30 Lakh.
