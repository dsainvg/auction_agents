BIOGRAPHY
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
   ïˆ² Ishan Kishan, a left-handed wicketkeeper-batter, has earned a reputation
  as one of India's cleanest hitters. Having led the India U19 team to a
  runners-up finish in the 2016 World Cup, Kishan debuted for Gujarat Lions in
  2016 and then moved to MI in 2018. With MI, Kishan helped them win the IPL
  2019 and 2020 titles, scoring 516 runs in IPL 2020. His stellar performance
  in 2022 saw him become the most expensive buy of the auction for INR 15.25
  Crore. Kishan, known for his big-hitting, even has a double century in ODIs
  to his name. For TATA IPL 2025, Sunrisers Hyderabad secured him for a
  massive INR 11.25 Crore, making him their most expensive player.
