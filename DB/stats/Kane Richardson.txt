--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

  Australia in recent times. This South Australia native made his first-class
  debut during the 2010-11 season and has represented the Adelaide Strikers in
  the Big Bash League. Signed by the Pune Warriors India for IPL 2013, he had
  the opportunity to sharpen his skills with the legendary Allan Donald. He
  was then inducted in the Rajasthan Royals side in the 2014 auction, followed
  by RCB in IPL 2016. The fourteenth edition of the league will be his second
  stint with the RCB.
