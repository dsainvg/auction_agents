BIOGRAPHY
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
    A player who can bat explosively, bowl off-spin, keep wickets, and field
  like a livewire, Glenn Phillips is a true multi-dimensional cricketer. The
  South Africa-born New Zealand international has been a consistent performer
  for the Black Caps, with a century in the T20 World Cup 2022 to his name. He
  also had IPL stints with Rajasthan Royals and Sunrisers Hyderabad. His
  recent Champions Trophy 2025 campaign, where he played a crucial role in New
  Zealand’s runners-up finish, further established his credentials, especially
  with his multiple one-handed stunners on the field. Gujarat Titans secured
  his services for INR 2 Crore at the 2025 TATA IPL Auction.
