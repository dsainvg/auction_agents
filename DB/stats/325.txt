--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
    Aniket Verma, a right-handed hard-hitting batter from Uttar Pradesh, made
  his mark in domestic cricket while playing for Madhya Pradesh. With an
  impressive record in the U23 circuit, Verma caught the attention of
  Sunrisers Hyderabad, who secured him for INR 30 Lakh at his base price for
  TATA IPL 2025.
