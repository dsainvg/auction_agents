BIOGRAPHY
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
    An explosive middle-order batter, Ashutosh Sharma made a name for himself
  in IPL 2024 with impactful cameos for Punjab Kings. In 11 matches, he
  smashed 189 runs at an impressive strike rate of 167.25. His ability to
  accelerate the scoring was evident when he recorded the fastest fifty by an
  Indian in T20 cricket, reaching the milestone in just 11 balls during the
  Syed Mushtaq Ali Trophy 2023. Delhi Capitals secured his services for INR
  3.8 Crore at the TATA IPL 2025 Auction.
